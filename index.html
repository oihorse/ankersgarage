Ankers Garage is a full service auto repair shop for your car or truck.

We are family owned and operated, located on Mt. Rose Avenue in the heart of York, PA. Our job is to ensure you receive top notch customer service and the best repair at the fairest price.

Having over 30 years in the auto repair business, we’ve worked on everything from domestic cars to exotic foreign imports. There isn’t a job we haven’t seen or repaired. You can feel comfortable bringing in your domestic or import.

We are licensed to do all testing and inspections for the state of Pennsylvania at Ankers Garage. Come down and visit us for your inspection needs for less than the other places are charging!

Our pipe bender means that we can do pretty much any custom exhaust work you may need in any size, shape, or configuration for any type vehicle. We can also handle all types of bodywork and repainting.
